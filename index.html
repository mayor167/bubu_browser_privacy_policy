About Bubu Browser

Bubu Browser is a modern lightweight browser and news aggregator designed by Jacombe Ventures / Oyeyemi Mayokun. Enjoy fast browsing, real-time news, sports updates, business headlines, entertainment, technology and more — all in one simple browsing experience.
